Army Community Service (ACS) at Camp Parks – A Brief Overview

For military families at Camp Parks, the Army Community Service (ACS) Relocation Assistance Program provides invaluable support during PCS. Located at Bldg. 312, 9th Street Camp Parks, CA 94568, with a commercial phone number 925-875-4388, the ACS helps streamline the relocation process for you and your loved ones. They are available Monday through Friday, from 7:30am to 4:30pm and also outside standard business hours by appointment. For additional resources, visit the Army Community Service website.

Additionally, you can find more in-depth details on our PCS guides and resources section, including a related link to Camp Parks.

Camp Parks Essentials

Camp Parks serves as a major hub for military training and operations, providing support for America's Army Reserve and other Department of Defense partners. It's essential to know that the region is characterized by a high cost of living, which is why it's wise to contact the PRFTA ACS at 925-875-4678 for financial planning assistance related to relocation.

The installation provides a wide array of services, from health care and housing to child and youth programs. Whether it's preparing for your move or settling in, Army Community Service is there to support every step of the way. To learn more about the critical mission and support services available, visit the official Camp Parks website or access Google Map for directions.

On-site Services and Support for Military Families

Military families at Camp Parks have access to a broad range of on-site services and support systems, including comprehensive housing options, educational programs, and health care services. The Military and Family Support Center can connect you with the necessary resources to balance military life successfully.

Moreover, the Morale, Welfare and Recreation network assists in integrating service members and their families into the community through various programs and activities that promote wellbeing and leisure.
