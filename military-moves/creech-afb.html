Understanding Creech AFB Relocation Assistance Program

The Military and Family Readiness Center (M&FRC) is your source for support during relocations at Creech AFB. Services such as pre-move planning, housing information, and spousal employment aid are available to make your transition as smooth as possible. The center is located at Building 24 Room 220, Second St Creech AFB, NV 89018, with commercial phone access at 702-404-0864. Operating hours span from Monday to Friday, 7:30 a.m. to 3:30 p.m., and closed on weekends and holidays. To ensure the best guidance, appointments should be scheduled during operational times.
